What is Cyberminds?

Cyberminds is a platform dedicated to educating others about cybersecurity.

How it works

To learn about cybersecurity you can click the Course button at the top of the website. There you will find 12 courses to choose from ranging in difficultly level. Once you click a course you will be presented with 2 options:

  • Option 1: Reading

  • Option 2: Activity

    • The reading will contain information about cybersecurity that you need in order to complete the activity. The Activity will have quizzes and games to test your knowledge.
