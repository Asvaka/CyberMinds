FAQs

Are the courses in cyberminds for free?

Yes! You can access all of our courses for free.

Do I need prior experience with programming?

Not necessarily. Many of our courses don't require you to code. However, there are some activities that need some programming knowledge, but it is not necessary that you complete these.

What topics are covered in this course?

A variety of topics are covered such as the CIA triad, encryption, penetration testing, cyber attacks and more.

How long will it take to complete the course?

The courses get more complex as you go, and your pace may be fast or slow. The amount of time varies per person.

How do I navigate through the courses?

You can choose one of the course cards on the courses page, and toggle through the table of contents section on the left to get through the courses. You can also use the buttons on the bottom of the page to move through the course.
